Privacy Policy - Bob Ross Image Replacer

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.

Local Storage

The extension only stores a simple enable/disable preference locally on your device. This data never leaves your computer.

Changes to YouTube

The extension only makes visual changes to YouTube pages and does not:

  • Interfere with YouTube's functionality
  • Collect data from YouTube
  • Modify user interactions or behavior tracking

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy if the extension's functionality changes. Users will be notified through the Chrome Web Store update process.
